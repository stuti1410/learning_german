### Points to Remember
`Woher` and `Wo` both mean 'Where'.
<table>
  <tr>
    <th>German</th>
    <th>Meaning</th>
    <th>Example</th>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>Wo</td>
    <td>Where (location)</td>
    <td>Wo wohnst du? → Where do you live?</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>Woher</td>
    <td>From where (origin)</td>
    <td>Woher kommst du? → Where are you from?</td>
  </tr>
</table>
